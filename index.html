The Problem

The way tendering works today
is costing you money.

Manual, email-based, gut-feel — every tender cycle wastes weeks and leaves value on the table.

BOQs re-entered by hand, every time

Contractors copy rates into spreadsheets. Your QS copies them again. Each re-entry introduces errors, gaps, and version control chaos before a single number is compared.

↑ Re-entry errors in every manual tender

Submissions by email — no control, no audit

Bids arrive in inboxes. Deadlines are disputed. Bids get seen before opening. There's no sealed process, no immutable record, and no defensible audit trail if challenged.

Zero auditability in standard email tendering

4–6 week cycles delay every decision

Chasing submissions, issuing addenda, waiting on late returners — the tender cycle stretches to six weeks. Decisions slip, programmes shift, and costs rise before a spade hits the ground.

4–6 weeks average tender cycle in UAE

Analysis by gut feel, not data

Rate comparisons are done manually — eyeballing a spreadsheet. There's no systematic outlier detection, no normalization, no historical benchmarks. Negotiation leaves money on the table.

Millions in undetected rate outliers per project

Platform Features

Everything your QS team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built around the workflows of developer-side QS practice — not a generic procurement tool.

Contractor Database

Build a pre-qualified pool of contractors — graded, tagged by discipline and region, ready to invite in seconds.

  • EOI / PQQ-lite onboarding flow
  • Contractor grades A / B / C per discipline
  • Compliance documents and certifications
  • Searchable by region, trade, and tier

Sealed Bid Governance

Cryptographically sealed submissions. Dual-approval opening. Immutable audit log from invitation to award.

  • Bids locked until exact opening date/time
  • No early access for any user or role
  • Auto-generated minutes of opening
  • Immutable, timestamped audit trail
  • Full separation of duties by role

Commercial Analysis Engine

AI-powered BOQ parsing, normalization, and comparison across all returns — producing a negotiation schedule a QS can actually use.

  • Multi-sheet BOQ parsing (handles real-world messiness)
  • Rate outlier detection and flagging
  • Per-item high/low spread analysis
  • Per-contractor negotiation schedules
  • Excel + PDF exports, award-ready
